    Housing

    First-Year Student Housing Residence halls are equipped with wireless internet, air conditioning, cable and comfortable furniture. Most feature suite-style living arrangements, with private bedrooms and a shared bathroom. All halls include free laundry facilities. Typically, first-year students live in these buildings.
